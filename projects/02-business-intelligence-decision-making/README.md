# Data Analytics and Organisational Decision Making

## Project Overview

This project explores how organisations can convert operational data into actionable business insight and better management decisions. It combines discussion of organisational analytics challenges with practical business intelligence and performance analysis.

## Business Problem

Organisations often collect large amounts of data across customers, products, sales, logistics, and operations but struggle to convert fragmented information into consistent, decision-ready insight.

## Objectives

- Analyse organisational performance using business intelligence techniques.
- Develop meaningful performance views and KPIs.
- Identify trends and patterns across products, customers, regions, sales, profit, discounts, shipping, and returns.
- Evaluate organisational barriers to successful analytics adoption.
- Translate analytical findings into management-focused insights.

## Analytical Approach

The project examines multiple dimensions of organisational performance, including:

- sales and profit trends;
- product and category performance;
- regional performance;
- customer segment analysis;
- state and city contribution;
- discount relationships;
- shipping mode analysis;
- return trends.

The analysis also considers broader analytics implementation challenges such as data quality, data integration, legacy systems, organisational adoption, and the need to connect dashboards with real decision processes.

## Business Value

The project demonstrates how BI can support performance management by helping decision-makers understand where value is created, where performance is deteriorating, and which areas require further investigation.

The analytical approach can support:

- executive reporting;
- sales performance management;
- profitability analysis;
- customer segmentation;
- regional performance reviews;
- operational KPI monitoring.

## Tools and Techniques

Power BI, data analysis, KPI development, business intelligence, performance reporting, trend analysis, segmentation, and data visualisation.

## Skills Demonstrated

KPI design, dashboard thinking, performance analysis, business insight communication, trend and variance analysis, data quality awareness, and organisational analytics strategy.

## Future Improvements

The project could be extended through automated data pipelines, a documented star schema, advanced DAX measures, forecasting, drill-through analysis, and alert-based exception reporting.
